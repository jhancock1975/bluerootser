Definitions[edit]

生

  1. gentleman
  2. student
    • 你作為全日制生必須定期上課。 [MSC, trad.]
      你作为全日制生必须定期上课。 [MSC, simp.]
      Nǐ zuòwèi quánrìzhì shēng bìxū dìngqī shàngkè. [Pinyin]
      As a full-time student, you should attend classes on a regular basis.
  3. actor or a male character, especially in Chinese operas
  4. Mr (see 先生)
  5. to give birth; to bear
    • 她妻子生了一對雙胞胎女孩。 [MSC, trad.]
      她妻子生了一对双胞胎女孩。 [MSC, simp.]
      Tā qīzǐ shēng le yī duì shuāngbāotāi nǚhái. [Pinyin]
      His wife gave birth to twin girls.
    • John 1.9
    • 那光是真光，照亮一切生在世上的人。 [MSC, trad. and simp.]
      Nà guāng shì zhēn guāng, zhàoliàng yīqiè shēng zài shìshàng de rén. [Pinyin]
      That was the true Light, which lighteth every man that cometh into the world.
  6. to be born
    • 他生在中國 / 他生在中国  ―  tā shēng zài zhōngguó  ―  He is born in China
  7. to grow
    • 紅豆生南國 / 红豆生南国  ―  hóngdòu shēng nán guó  ―  Red berries grow in southern lands.
  8. to light (a fire)
    • 母親沒有生爐子。 [MSC, trad.]
      母亲没有生炉子。 [MSC, simp.]
      Mǔqīn méi yǒu shēng lúzǐ. [Pinyin]
      Mother did not light the stove.
  9. raw; uncooked
    • 我不喜歡在我色拉裡的生洋蔥。 [MSC, trad.]
      我不喜欢在我色拉里的生洋葱。 [MSC, simp.]
      Wǒ bù xǐhuān zài wǒ sèlā lǐ de shēng yángcōng. [Pinyin]
      I do not like raw onions in my salad.
